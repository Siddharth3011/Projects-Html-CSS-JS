📘 User Guide

🔍 Searching for Weather

To get weather information, simply enter the name of an Indian city (e.g., Delhi, Mumbai, Ladakh) into the search bar and click "Search".

🌤 What You Get
  • Current temperature, feels like, min/max temperatures
  • Humidity, pressure, visibility
  • Wind speed, sunrise and sunset time
  • Region-specific travel tips
🌙 Dark/Light Theme

Click the 🌗 button in the navbar to toggle between light and dark mode for a better viewing experience.

🧭 Best Usage Tips
  • Use city names like "Delhi", "Ladakh", or "Goa".
  • Make sure you are connected to the internet.
  • App works best on modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Edge).
