🏞 Top Travel Destinations in India

Ladakh

Known for its cold deserts, scenic mountains, and Buddhist monasteries. Weather is often cold even in summer.

  • ❄️ Wear layered warm clothing
  • 🧴 Use sunscreen at high altitudes
  • 💧 Stay hydrated due to dryness

Goa

A tropical coastal paradise with beaches and nightlife. Summers can be hot and humid.

  • 🧢 Carry hats and sunglasses
  • 🧴 Apply sunscreen frequently
  • 🌊 Great for water sports (avoid monsoon)

Shimla

A hill station famous for snowfall and colonial architecture. Winters are freezing cold.

  • 🧣 Pack heavy woolens in winter
  • ⚠️ Roads may be slippery in snow
  • ☀️ Pleasant in spring and autumn

Rajasthan (Jaipur, Jaisalmer)

Known for deserts, forts, and royal heritage. Summers are extremely hot.

  • 🔥 Visit during winter months (Oct–Feb)
  • 🌡️ Light cotton clothing recommended
  • 💦 Keep water handy while exploring

Kerala

Popular for backwaters and greenery. Weather is usually warm and humid, especially in monsoon.

  • ☔ Carry umbrella or raincoat in monsoon
  • 🐊 Be cautious during boat rides
  • 🌿 Best visited between Nov–Feb
